Ravioli Lasagna

One of my favorite recipes is Ravioli Lasagna.

  • Marinara Sauce
  • Frozen Cheese Ravioli
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Basil Leaves
  1. Spread half of a jar of marinara sauce
  2. Add half of the ravioli
  3. Sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese over the sauce
  4. Bake the lasagna for 30 min
  5. Garnish with bazil
